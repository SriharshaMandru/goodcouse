    Good Course - Learning
    Website Template

    By Waves Design


    Good Course is a top-notch Webflow Template designed for anyone who wants to
    launch their course with a professional online presence. It comes with a full CMS
    and Ecommerce setup so you can start selling courses right away.

    Good Course is a top-notch Webflow Template designed for anyone who
    wants to launch their course with a professional online presence. With its
    beautiful and user-friendly design, it's easy for people to find courses and
    make purchases.


    Good Course comes with a clean CMS and Ecommerce setup, making it a
    breeze to upload your content and start selling courses quickly. Inside the
    template, you'll discover Blog and Teacher CMS collections, making it simple
    to share knowledge and create a teacher's profile. The Ecommerce features
    are tailor-made for selling courses, with fields for course descriptions,
    teacher references, and everything you need to get your course up and
    running.


    If you're thinking about creating your online course website, Good Course is
    the obvious choice!


    One of the standout features of Good Course Template is the included Figma
    file. This is a great resource if you're planning to hire a Webflow specialist to
    customize your website, as they will be able to easily explore new design
    options using the predefined styles. To get your hands on the Figma file,
    simply email us at templates@wavesdesign.io with your order confirmation
    and we'll be happy to share it with you.



    Good Course template features:


    Responsive-

    Good Course is optimized to offer a frictionless
    experience on every screen. No matter how you combine our sections,
    they will look good on desktop, tablet, and phone.


    Premium, custom, simply great -

    yes, we know... it's easy to say it, but that's the fact. We did put a lot of
    thought into the template. Good Course was designed by an award-
    winning designer. Layouts you will find in our template are custom made
    to fit the industry after carefully made research.


    Optimised for speed -

    we used our best practices to make sure your new website loads fast.
    All of the images are compressed to have as little size as possible.
    Whenever possible we used vector formats - the format made for the web.


    Reusable animations -

    both complex and simple animations are an inseparable element of
    modern website. We created our animations in a way that can be easily
    reused even by Webflow beginners.


    Modular -

    our template is modular, meaning you can combine different sections as
    well as single elements, like buttons, images, etc. with each other without
    losing on consistency of the design. Long story short, different elements
    will always look good together.


    100% customisable -

    on top of being modular Good Course Template was created using the
    best Webflow techniques, like: global Color Swatches, reusable classes,
    components and more.


    Content Management System (CMS) -

    Good Course includes a blog and teachers collections that are made on
    the powerful Webflow CMS. This will let you add new content extremely
    easily.


    ECommerce -

    Good Course comes with eCommerce set up, so you can start selling
    your courses straight away.


    Documented -

    We want to offer you the best experience when working on your new
    website. In the template you can find general information about editing
    Webflow sites.
